Getting started

How to start with BrainSINS integration

  • Create an Account
  • Integrate in 5 Minutes
  • Manage and Optimize

1. Create your BrainSINS Account

Before integrating BrainSINS, you need to create a BrainSINS user account. You can create your account from our commercial website.

Create your Account

2. Integrate in 5 Minutes

If you make use of Prestashop, Magento or Shopify, you can integrate BrainSINS in less than 5 minutes using our plugins.

But if you don't use any of this popular eCommerce CMS, don't be afraid, our JavaScript integration is really easy and you can integrate BrainSINS in a few hours.

3. Manage and Optimize

After integrating BrainSINS, you are ready to start selling more. From our management console, you can define your personalization strategies and measure its results.

Start learning how to configure the look & feel of the product recommendations, so they match your actual design.
